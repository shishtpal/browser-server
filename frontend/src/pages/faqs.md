# Frequently Asked Questions

## What is this application for?

This is a simple TODO application to help you manage your daily tasks.

## How do I add a new task?

Simply type your task in the input field at the top of the page and click the "Add" button.

## How do I delete a task?

Click the "Delete" button next to the task you want to remove.

## Is this application free to use?

Yes, this application is completely free to use.
